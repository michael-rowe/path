Leading teaching innovation across the programme

Ready Evidence-based Updated 20 May 2026

Over the past two years I have led the redesign of how clinical reasoning is taught across the pre-registration physiotherapy programme, shifting from lecture-led delivery to a simulation-anchored model evaluated against student outcomes and external benchmarks.

What I did

I convened a cross-module working group, secured time-release for three colleagues, and ran two iterative pilots before full roll-out. Each iteration was evaluated with module feedback, attainment data, and structured peer observation against the teaching quality framework.

“A genuinely different experience — I finally understood how to think like a clinician, not just recall facts.” — Year 2 student, module evaluation

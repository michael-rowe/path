# Personal statement: |^|

> **tip** **What this is.** A short narrative — written in your voice, addressed to an assessor — that introduces your case before they read the claims. Two pages of focused argument is plenty. Signpost the strongest evidence rather than re-stating it.

## Summary of contribution

Two or three sentences naming the through-line of your work. What is the case you are making, in one paragraph?

## Approach to the framework

How does your career and current practice align with the framework you are submitting against (teaching excellence, scholarship, leadership, citizenship, or whatever the equivalent is)?

## Looking forward

What do you intend to do over the next 3–5 years that builds credibly on this record?

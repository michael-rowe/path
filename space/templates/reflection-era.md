# ERA reflection: |^|

## Experience

The event, situation, or activity. What happened? Who was involved? What was the context?

## Reflection

What did the experience mean? What sense did you make of it at the time, and what sense do you make of it now? What assumptions or beliefs were challenged?

## Action

What will you do differently? What concrete steps follow from the reflection? How will you know whether the action has worked?

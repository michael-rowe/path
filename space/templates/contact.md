# ${pageDisplayName()}

## Context

|^|How do you know them? What is the nature of your professional relationship? Two or three sentences answering: *why is this person in my network?*

## Their work

What are they working on? What do they care about? Update this when you learn something new — keep it specific enough to be useful in future conversation.

## Expertise

What they are known for, professionally. Bullet list of topic areas, methodologies, or specialisms.

-

## Shared interests

Topics, problems, or questions you both care about. The basis for genuine, recurring conversation rather than one-off transactions.

-

## Collaboration opportunities

Where might you work together? **What could you offer them — without being asked?** A five-minute favour, an introduction, a piece of writing they would find useful, a question worth asking. Lead with generosity; the network grows from value given, not value extracted.

## Personal notes

Only what helps you connect authentically — communication preferences, interests outside work, things they have mentioned that mattered to them. Skip trivia. Keep it spare and human.

## History

Append a dated note after each interaction. Brief is fine — enough to remember context. Update `last_contact` and `next_contact` in the right-hand panel as you go.

# |^|Type Rolfe reflection title here

## What? *(descriptive)*

A description of the event. What is the problem, situation, or experience? What was your role? What were the consequences? What were you trying to achieve?

## So what? *(theoretical)*

What does this tell you about you, your patients/students/colleagues, the model of practice you are using, your theoretical assumptions? What broader issues arise? Bring in evidence and theory.

## Now what? *(action-oriented)*

What do you need to do to improve the situation, develop your practice, or address the broader implications? What are the consequences of acting — or not acting? Concrete steps, with success criteria.

# Driscoll reflection: |^|

## What?

A description of the event. What happened, when, who was involved, what was the trigger for noticing it?

## So what?

What is the meaning? Why does this matter — for you, for others, for practice? What did the experience reveal?

## Now what?

What follows? What needs to change, be tried, be tested, or be communicated? What is the concrete next step, and what would tell you it had worked?

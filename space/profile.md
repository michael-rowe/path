## About this page

Your **profile** is the single source of professional information that Path uses when building any PDF export — the cover page, the page header, the assessor-facing summary.

Edit the fields in the right-hand **Inspector** panel to add your details. At minimum, fill in **Full name** and **Job title** — these appear on every document you export.

The Bio field appears on the export cover page. Write two or three sentences describing your professional background and current role.

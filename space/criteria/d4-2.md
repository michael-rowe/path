# D4.2 — Sustained, effective and strategic leadership in academic practice and academic development

> Descriptor 4: Principal Fellow

This is the heart of D4. The criterion wants **outcomes**, not roles. *Position held* is not evidence of *strategic leadership*; the latter is shown by what changed because of your leadership — for learners, for colleagues, for the practice of others. The Quantified Impact section of each claim is where this distinction is made or lost.

## Indicators

- Strategic leadership of teaching and learning at school, faculty, institutional, or sector level
- Sustained record of leading change in academic practice with measurable impact on learners and/or colleagues
- Leadership of significant academic development initiatives (CPD, induction, mentoring, faculty development)
- Evidence of impact on academic practice *beyond* your immediate role and remit
- Strategic leadership demonstrated through outcomes, not just position held

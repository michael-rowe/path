# D4.1 — Active commitment to and championing of all Professional Values

> Descriptor 4: Principal Fellow

The criterion is satisfied not by demonstrating *your own* commitment to the Professional Values, but by demonstrating that the values are *consistently adopted* across the academic practice you influence. Evidence at the level of one teaching role is too narrow; evidence at the level of policies, mentoring relationships, communities of practice, or public advocacy is the right scale.

## Indicators

- Sustained championing of V1 — Respect individual learners and diverse learning communities
- Sustained championing of V2 — Promote engagement in learning and equity of opportunity
- Sustained championing of V3 — Use scholarship, reflection, and evidence-informed approaches
- Sustained championing of V4 — Respond to the wider context in which HE operates
- Sustained championing of V5 — Collaborate with others to enhance practice
- Championing through institutional policy work, mentoring, communities of practice, or public advocacy
- Evidence the values are *consistently adopted* across the practice you influence — not just present in your own

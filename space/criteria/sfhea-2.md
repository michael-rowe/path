# D3.2 — Wider development through engagement in activities that support, enhance, and/or recognise practice

> Descriptor 3: Senior Fellow

D3.2 is about engagement *beyond* your immediate teaching role — scholarship, external networks, peer review, resource creation, and recognition of others' practice. It captures the ways you support and enhance teaching and learning through activities that extend your reach. D3.2 activities feed the causal chain that D3.3 must close: if you cannot show that D3.2 work influenced practice or student learning, the chain is incomplete.

## Indicators

- Scholarship of teaching and learning that informs or is adopted by others' practice
- Engagement with professional bodies, networks, or sector organisations to support others' development
- Peer review of teaching or curriculum — both giving and organising it
- Development of resources, frameworks, or tools that colleagues adopt
- Collaborative curriculum design or cross-institutional curriculum partnerships
- Recognition activities: awards, nominations, or formal acknowledgement of others' practice
- External recognition of your own developmental activity (invited speaker, external examiner, reviewer)

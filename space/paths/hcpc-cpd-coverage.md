## Notes on what each standard wants

- **Standard 1** is satisfied by the CPD record itself (this Path), so a "missing claim" here usually just means you haven't yet written a meta-claim describing your record.
- **Standard 2** is the most evidence-hungry — a *mixture* of activity types is the operative word. A profile of only conferences, or only formal courses, is weak.
- **Standard 3** wants explicit contribution-to-practice — claims should connect activity → change in your practice → benefit observed.
- **Standard 4** is about service-user benefit specifically. This is the standard most often under-evidenced; reflections should name the user benefit, not just the practice change.
- **Standard 5** is procedural — confirms you'd produce the profile if asked. Once the system is set up, this is satisfied by definition.

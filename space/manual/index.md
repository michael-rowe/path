# The Path manual

Welcome. Path is a system for keeping a serious record of your professional development — the activities, reflections, and evidence you can later assemble into a submission for promotion, fellowship, or professional registration.

This manual is the place to come when you are not sure what to do next. It is written for **professionals who want their CPD to count**, not for software users.

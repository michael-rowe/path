# The Path manual

Path is a system for keeping a serious record of your professional development — the activities, reflections, and evidence you can later assemble into a submission for promotion, fellowship, or professional registration.

The manual is the place to come when you're not sure what to do next. It's written for **professionals who want their CPD to count**, not for software users.

## Start here

Open **[[manual/cheatsheet]]** — the quick reference page. It links to every other section of the manual, including a short reading list for first-time users.

> **tip** You can always come back here from the **Manual** link in the Navigator. There is no exam.
